Chauvinist
/ˈʃoʊvɪnɪst/
noun
Instructions: Click on any highlighted word in the definition below to see its meaning.
1.
A chauvinist is an individual with an unreasonable, excessive, or blind belief in the superiority of their own specific group—such as their gender, nationality, or race—often accompanied by contempt for others.
